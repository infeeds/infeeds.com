Even as the weather gets warmer and restrictions being lifted means we can see more of our friends, it's still completely acceptable to want the occasional night in.

With this in mind, here is everything you need for a great night in. Think food, comfies, and duvets.

1. A ridiculous, over the top drink

Whether you're a milkshake fan, or a hot chocolate fanatic, a great night in starts with a ridiculous everything-but-the-kitchen-sink drink. Your favorite flavor - topped with whipped cream, fruit, syrups, sweets, and anything else you can think of - will set you up for a good time. Because who doesn't love a sugar rush?

For something a little more sophisticated, try your hand at making some delicious cocktails for you and your friends.

2. Something for the cravings

Of course, no night in is complete without snacks or takeout. Use these Uber Eats Promo Code to grab yourself some grub and leave the washing up for tomorrow. But don't forget, even with dinner sorted, you'll still need to get yourself some snacks too...

3. Your favorite film

If you're someone who can watch the same film or TV show over and over again, you should have no trouble finding the perfect film for your night in. If you're struggling for something new and fresh, try Netflix Roulette and leave the decision up to someone else. You never know what you'll end up watching!

4. Other Entertainment

Watching TV isn't the only thing you can do on a night in. In fact, doing something other than the ordinary should be encouraged! Crafts are a fun way to spend a night with friends; making candles, creating pottery, or even just practicing a little painting can be a great way to destress on the weekend or after work.

5. A little self-care

Whether you treat yourself to a clean shave or have a pamper with a face mask and foot spa, taking time out to practice a little self-care is what a night in is all about. Try make your own natural mask, or pick up your favorite brands at the drug store.

6. Your softest PJs

You can't have a night in without your softest, comfiest clothes. Whether that's PJs or just an old pair of joggers, it's time to get snug. Nights in are all about relaxing and taking advantage of the full comforts you have around you.

7. A calming aroma

A lot of people recommend baking vanilla essence to create a wonderful smelling home. So whether you try this trick, or use it as an opportunity to shop for a new candle, make sure the house is smelling and feeling super relaxing.

These are just the basics you need for a cozy duvet night or day. If you fancy company, grab your favourite friends and organize a PJ party or games night. Nights in are a great way of saving money while still having a good time. Plus, you never know where the night might take you!
