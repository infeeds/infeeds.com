When you have finally found your dream girl on your lesbian dating app, you must think of perfect ways to bond with your girl to get to know her better. Finding out as much as you can about her outside your app is one of the ways you can see if she is The One. No amount of online interaction will compensate for real-life experiences that reveal the real character of the person you fancy. 

Consider these unique heartwarming ideas for your first lesbian date. This is a make or break date that will determine if you shall continue to foster an in-depth relationship or if you should move on.

Go on a Bowling Bonanza

Take your girl on a bowling bonanza. See who can hit the most strikes or spares. Bowling is a great activity that takes out the awkward feeling of first date encounters. You can easily discuss forms, talk about your past bowling experiences, and share about your gaming strategies. You can even show her how to throw the bowl so it won’t go into the gutter. 

Because this is a competitive sport, you will see how your girl reacts in the face of defeat. How people handle failure is very telling of their character. On top of that, bowling is an awesome all-in-one activity because you can also eat your meals within the same facility. 

Take an Arcade Challenge 

Go old school and take her on a traditional arcade gaming arena. Lesbian dating doesn’t have to be all frigid and serious. It is perfectly alright to let your hair down and have fun in an arcade with its bevvy of games. You can both enjoy the classic hammer console, Tekken, slider match, pinball, and more. This is a wonderful bonding moment for both of you, which will give you something to talk about when it’s time to eat dinner. 

Just like bowling, some games will require you to battle each other. This will test your date’s competitive spirit, and you will get a glimpse of how she handles a loss, which reveals her real personality. 

Enjoy a Day at an Amusement Park

Let your inner child come to life with an enjoyable day at an amusement park. While there, you can talk about your past childhood experiences. You can share your funny amusement park stories. 

Evaluate all the rides, and discuss why some are your favourites. An amusement park is a complete dating approach because you have food, entertainment, drinks, and souvenirs in one venue. It is the perfect first date venue because you can spend the whole day with the girl of your dreams from sunrise to sunset. 

Shoot It Out in Laser Tag

Another fun activity is shooting it out in laser tag. You will be playing with other teammates, so you can partner up with your date as you try to blast your opponents away. Aside from being fun, you can come up with strategies and see how well you work with each other as a team. Laser tag is an excellent way to see if you have chemistry. If you can survive a thrilling adventure in the dark, chances are you will be able to go through so much more with ease. 

Coming up with a unique first date is vital because it sets the tone of your relationship. Don’t be like the rest who only plan less-than-stellar first dates. Why settle for the ordinary when you can stand out and wow your partner.
