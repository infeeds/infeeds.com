The modern era has had an impact over many and all possible celebrations ranging from religious to historic. Be it baby showers, pre-wedding photoshoots, celebrating Halloween or a grand Christmas, everything has incorporated a twist. Not even Karwa Chauth could make the cut. Initially, Karwa Chauth was observed as a day for women to celebrate and pray for the longevity of their husbands' lives. Women kept fasts for their husbands but have now been joined by men as well. More number of men are being seen joining in the journey of their wives. They pay their respects to their wives' sacrifice and also pray for their long life together. It still is recalled as a festival for women, and they indeed do all religious activities, but men stay hungry and thirsty to lessen their wives' hurt.

What may seem impossible to many is conquered by women with grace and elegance each year. Marking the day for their husband's long life and prosperity, women take no sip of water or a gulp of food to please the gods to grant a cherished life to their husbands. Hindu calendars are overwhelmed with celebrations of auspicious festivities like these, but Karwa Chauth holds the highest place in the hierarchy.  

Karva Chauth sprays upon gifts and celebrations and memorable times. Unleash the day with a twist this year, and we have some ideas for you. While the Karwa Chauth thali is auspicious and part of a ritual, it is entitled to both simplicity and creativity. Well, a lavish thali may include diya, Kalash, roli etc. you can excite it with some modern-day items available in a wide variety in the market. 

But when it comes to a Karwa Chauth gift a ton of ideas and surprises flow in. And that's what we are here to help with.

  • Bunch of flowers to go 

No day starts better without a bunch of roses and lilies. What may not cost you a hefty amount will undoubtedly cost you plenty of emotions, and the return on investment is enormous. Showering love and emotions in the room will brighten up your day.
 

  • A cake perhaps

While your wife may not be able to eat it at the time, she can click some fantastic pictures and post them on her social media. Who doesn't like to boast a good family time? A post-dinner dessert is sitting still in your refrigerator too. 

  • An over the top surprise party

In the busy and hectic lives of the new age era, who needs reasons to celebrate? Celebrating free time and free minds are the new thing. Use this day to the fullest and plan a bonanza cum party for you and your family. With not much to do just sitting and enjoying life to the fullest will treat you the best. 

A big part of a millennial day goes into thinking what to eat next. And with that trouble not howling over your head anymore, you can divert your mind and think of better things to do. Free as a bird and light-minded as you will be, make this Karwa Chauth count like no other. Give yourself the gift of time topped with some presents of course.
