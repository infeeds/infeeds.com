Getting away for a holiday, vacation or short-haul trip is often the tonic you need after a grueling few weeks in the workplace. Having these little milestones to look forward to throughout the year enables you to work harder in order to make your way towards these vacations, and sets aside some time for you to enjoy making memories with your nearest and dearest. 

In this article, you’ll explore no less than five options that are as unique as they are dazzling, helping you plan your next family getaway in 2020.

Road Trips

When you watch movies about modern-day adventure, many of them begin in a car. Whether that’s in an open-top sports car revving off into the mountains, or a larger family vehicle traveling across the country in search of adventure, it’s a mystical image that’s firmly planted in many minds across the world. You can secure your home on wheels from Auto Finance Online in order to finance and prepare your own getaway, on the roads, into the great unknown. 

Hiking and Camping

For families who are a little tired of the suburban lifestyle in which they are so comfortable, there’s nothing like a shock to the system and the refresh of the mind that can be achieved with a camping trip. Getting your backpack on and shouldering the responsibilities for where you sleep at night, what you cook, and how you get there, is a great way of building bonds between family members, as well as enjoying the peaceful and magical nature of the wilderness. 

Airbnb City Trips

With the advent of booking websites that help you get to a new city and live in a comfortable new home with ease, it’s well and truly time for families to make the most of this new technology. If you’re running a little short of vacation time, but you’d still like to spend a few long weekends away this year, this is your best option. Find yourself a city that you can get to easily, and book an Airbnb where your family can relax. Then head out to explore all the family-friendly attractions — and on the cheap, too. 

Eco Lodges

If your family is concerned about the looming climate crisis, then an eco-lodge could be the perfect place to take everyone on a summer vacation. Made from renewable materials and using solar energy to warm the water and power the lights, these buildings show children what is possible without emitting tons of carbon into the atmosphere each minute of the day. 

Theme Parks

From Disneyland to Sea World, there are hundreds of exciting and thrilling theme parks spattered across the USA, all of which have lodgings for families to stay on the brink of the action. This option is one that your children will be particularly excited by, and you’ll enjoy guiding them around some of the world’s best family facilities, making memories on rides and rollercoasters. 

These five trip tips are essential for families looking for a unique getaway to plan in 2020.
