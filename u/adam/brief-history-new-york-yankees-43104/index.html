The New York Yankees are one of the biggest names in the sport of baseball known not only in the United States of America but worldwide. In fact, their famous NY hats can be seen donned by the fashion-conscious across the globe. 


The history of the club runs deeper than this though, and from an all-star team of baseball’s finest athletes to one of the most famous grounds in the history of baseball, the Yankees hold an impressive history of the game. 


Read on and find out more. 


When was the New York Yankees Stadium Built


Soon to be celebrating its centenary, the New York Yankees Stadium was constructed at a cost of, $2.5 million dollars way back in 1923, highlighting the city’s long-held devotion to the sport. When it was opened, almost a hundred years ago, the Yankees Stadium was the pinnacle of baseball at the time and was known as the most glamorous stadium in the country to visit for fans of the sport. 


Find out more about the Yankees world series championships and discover some interesting facts about the game from Imagine Sports. 


Famous Faces of The Club?


The New York Yankees have an undisputed pedigree when it comes to the sport of baseball and their hallowed grounds have been the home of numerous heroes of the sport. Babe Ruth, Joe DiMaggio, and Lou Gehrig, all now members of the mythical baseball hall of fame, built their legendary baseball careers from this famed location in the capital of the country. 


Find out more about some of the biggest moments in New York Yankees history from Bleacher Report.


The New York Yankees Today


The game is still immensely popular in the modern city of New York and attracts a huge number of visitors from at home and around the world every year. In 2009, the original stadium was replaced with something more suitable to the modern game at a cost of almost $2.5 billion. 


Able to cater to growing numbers of fans but still retaining the look and feel of the historic stadium, the Yankees have been able to hold on to their heritage while looking forward. 


Discover the latest happening from The Yankees website for the most up-to-the-minute news on the team, the stadium, and the glorious game.


The Yankees or Redsox?


Of course, a sports team is nothing without a good rivalry to get the blood flowing and the Yankees are no exception. One of the most fearsome rivalries in the sport of baseball is battled out by The New York Yankees and The Boston Redsox. 


These two teams have been at each other’s throats for almost a century and the heat does not look set to die down soon. Based just a stone’s throw away, The Red Sox have also had a hugely successful history in Baseball and their team has achieved some of the true feats of the sport. 


In short, The New York Yankees are one of the most famous names in the sport of baseball and for good reason, they’ve achieved huge success in the nation’s favorite game and reached almost legendary status amongst adoring fans worldwide. 


Let’s hope we see another 100 years of New York Yankees’ success.
