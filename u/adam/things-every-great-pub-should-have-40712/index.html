Things a great pub should have

Assorted drinks

People always love to share drinks at pubs and it is one of the things that people are attracted to the pub. Great pubs need to have a variety of assorted liquors from whiskeys, wines, champagnes, to even just beer or juice. When people have their choice of drink, they tend to stay longer.

Live music

Apart from drinking or having something nice to eat, good live music is another important thing that makes a pub great. People do not always look forward to good food when they have gone out but great music as well. It is even better when the music is live.

Baby changing facilities

Pubs are places which nursing moms equally do visit from time to time with their babies. These amazing babies getting wet all-natural and moms do need facilities where the babies can be changed comfortably.

Nice and super clean loos

Having a clean loo is equally another necessity that makes a great pub. The hygienic state is one thing people always check out when they visit a pub. Thus it always needs to be sparkling clean and odor free.

The best pub in Melbourne has a good majority of these activities and facilities as well as many others.

Beer gardens

Some people do love their outdoor space and will not hesitate to chill out when they do find that outdoor space in a pub. This is not like a compulsory factor but having this will be super great and make people appreciate the beauty of life.

Dominos and other games

Games sure do keep people entertained and it will be nice to find dominos games or games that can be quite engaging and competitive to play without being quite strenuous. Having this category of games in the pub sure does make it great.

Cinemas

Watching a good movie over some snacks and dreams is sure a nice way to spend some quality hours in a pub. A great pub should equally have a mail list, library or even shops around.

Huge sporting tournaments

If you cannot be present in a particular field to watch a game live, the next place where the match could be watched in a fun and lively place will be a pub. It is always funny how strangers get to click and share so much about the ongoing tournament which always turns out to be more hilarious.
