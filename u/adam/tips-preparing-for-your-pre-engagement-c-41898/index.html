In all cultures across India, the engagement or ring ceremony is an essential pre-wedding ritual. This event brings together families of the future bride and groom to peacefully share words of wisdom and offer pledges of support for the couple. Historically, this ritual involves elaborate ceremonies and fancy details.

At present, Indian couples still value this vital ritual while adding more modern elements. They always pay homage to family tradition. One of the critical aspects of this event is the exchange of rings between the groom and bride to be. Couples must heed how to buy platinum ring guidelines to select the best ones that will represent their undying love.

Here are other tips that would help couples achieve a memorable ring ceremony:

  • Choose Platinum Rings

In the past, gold dominated the jewelry scene. But platinum, a precious metal, is fast becoming the material of choice for most modern couples. This "metal of kings" is an excellent choice for several important reasons:

  • Platinum’s color does not lighten or fade

A platinum's shine does not fade away over time. It beautifully represents how a husband and wife's love for each other should be. Real platinum does not require re-plating, unlike white gold. 

  • Platinum is the Purest Metal for Jewelry

Platinum is the only material for jewelry that is used in its purest form. Since it is a hard metal, there is no need to mix them with other metals. 

  • Platinum is Hypoallergenic

Platinum does not irritate the skin, no matter how long the wearer uses it. It is perfect for people with sensitive skin and other allergies to metals.

  • Platinum is Durable and Strong

This dense metal is durable by nature and is ideal for an engagement ring that is often used every day. Each intricate detail will forever stay the same, just like on the day it was made.

Platinum rings are also known to be very strong. Its prongs will never loosen nor break. Many how-to buy platinum ring guidelines emphasize checking the rings for authenticity. In India, there is a Pt 950 marked that is always present in platinum jewelry.

  • Prepare Your Ring Ceremony Tray

The ring or engagement ceremony tray is an essential part of the ritual. You may order your trays or go the DIY route. Whichever you choose, make sure that the tray follows the wedding theme or color. Since the exchange of rings is the highlight of the event, they need to be nestled in a beautiful vessel that matches the couple’s personality.

Some trays include photos, flowers, beads, gems, or colorful ribbons. Other couples, on the other hand, choose a silver tray to carry their symbol of love.

Traditionally, engagement ring trays are colorful and elaborate, but brides and grooms of the present generation are now choosing more subdued designs. It allows the star of the show, the rings, to stand out.

  • Discuss the traditions of both families

Various regions in India have their rituals and traditions when it comes to exchanging rings. It would be great to discuss with your partner how to go about the event. The ring ceremony is the start of a lifelong commitment, and it would be beneficial for everyone to start at the right foot. 

 

The pre-engagement is usually a festive and colorful celebration that marks the beginning of the couple's married life. Exchanging platinum rings make it more unique and will symbolize a lasting and healthy relationship.
