The COVID-19 pandemic threats are still around. However, governments from all over the world cannot keep strict lockdown rules without causing further economic implications anymore. That is why they begin lifting such rules and implement regulations to curb the spread of New Coronavirus in the new norm.

The same goes for your fitness gym as well. You can’t keep your business closed for a longer time, can you? Thus, prepare your Zogics gym wipes supply along with other disinfectants, and take note of these important reminders to keep COVID-19 from spreading in your gym.

Tips in keeping COVID-19 from wreaking havoc in your fitness gym

1. Follow the basic recommendations from health experts

Begin by applying basic suggestions from health professionals. Buy enough disinfectant with 60 to 70% alcohol composition, wash hands thoroughly for 2 minutes several times a day, and use personal protective equipment or PPE. Then, create a doable action plan for your team to follow with the help of the next tips.

2. Follow efficient cleaning methods

You should clean your gym, but you should do it efficiently with quality sanitation as a big goal. Thoroughly disinfect every spot in your facilities and on your equipment, especially high-touch surfaces, using recommended agents. Be sure to read the labels of each disinfectant too. Each may have different usage procedures, such as the length of time you should let it sit before rinsing.

Also, clean equipment in one stroke, especially when you’re using gym wipes like Zogics. Back and forth or circular motion potentially pushes germs back to the same spot. It is important to avoid using the same piece of wipe more than once as well.

After cleaning, dispose of used PPE properly, as well as wash your hands according to expert suggestions.

3. Make regular disinfecting and sanitizing schedules

Ideally, you should clean your entire gym and its equipment thoroughly when members are out. But you can do regular disinfecting during operation hours too. This is to keep equipment free from germs, especially after a sweaty workout session. Remember that not all members clean after their selves, so it is best to be sure.

4. Make disinfectants and sanitizers available all over the gym

Be sure to make enough disinfectants available such as towels, gym wipes, and sanitizers. This is to keep disinfectants near your members, which increases the likelihood of them using it. It also makes disinfecting easier for your staff, since they don’t need to hit the storage area frequently.

5. Educate everybody accordingly

Lastly, properly educate everybody in your fitness gym with these protocols. This helps keep everything in order, especially when people know how to help prevent the spread of COVID-19 inside.

Moreover, conduct regular meetings for your staff, then track their cleaning processes accordingly. This is your best chance of making sure that they’re following your new-norm rules. Also, give them enough authority to talk with members about such rules, especially when somebody makes a violation.


Be creative when educating members too. You can’t pull them all in a meeting, so invite them to a sort of seminar or make some educational brochures. Putting some signage for reminders all over the gym is also great.


These are only a few valuable tips in keeping COVID-19 from spreading in your gym facilities. It is still better to talk with your local sanitation offices for more info and guide. Also, buy enough disinfectants such as Zogics gym wipes, and make them available in your fitness gym.
