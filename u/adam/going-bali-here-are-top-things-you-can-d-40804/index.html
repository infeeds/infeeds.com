If you are looking for a vacation that bridges culture, diversity, and exciting adventures, then look no further than Bali. The iconic Indonesian island is a backpackers haven, a tropical and dreamy expanse with a myriad of destinations and enough fun to last a lifetime. With the topics summer closing in, book your tickets, and prepare for an adventure that will etch memories into your heart forever.


There are a ton of things you can do in sunny Bali, so you must plan and formulate an itinerary that will make the most out of your time. With the diversity of the islands sights, you can enjoy a bit of everything, from a luxe pool villa in Bali to an underwater adventure and temple-hopping day trip. Here are the top picks for your upcoming Balinese vacation.


Luxury Stays


Bali is the affordable cousin to some of the worlds most luxurious tropics, but it does not skimp on its fair share of upscale living. The island is full of posh private villas with unbelievable sights and celebrity-approved amenities. With premium linens, artsy interior, custom-crafted toiletries, or in-house gastronomic experiences, you might feel tempted to stay indoors for the majority of your trip.


A pool villa in Bali will complement the exciting outdoors with an interior where you can relax and recharge for the next days events. Nowhere else in the world will you find architectural wonders made of Balinese stones, bamboo, and other indigenous materials. If you book early, you will be able to snag some of the highest-end options before the high seasons price increase.


Temple Sights


While most people know Bali for its breathtaking beaches, the island also houses wonders on land. Go on a spiritual day trip by taking in temple sights. From the Pura Taman Ayun to the Ulun Danu Temple, you will enjoy the iconic high-rise Hindu temples surrounded by greenery and water. Explore the islands spiritual elements, which have been preserved for hundreds of years and have found their way in the list of UNESCO sites. Later, visit nearby art museums to complete a culturally-enriching day trip that will reinforce your love for Bali.


Watersports


Lying on the beach all day is fun, but nothing sparks more excitement than a heart-pumping rush of adrenaline-filled activities. Look toward Balis horizons, and you will find sceneries framed by water, so grab the opportunity to enjoy some watersports. For thrill-seekers, go jet skiing and parasailing along Balis gorgeous landscapes. If you are visiting with kids, ride the rolling donut or the banana boat for a dose of kid-friendly fun. And for merfolk and marine enthusiasts, you will have a blast snorkelling and scuba diving along with the islands coral and fish population.


Waterfall Tour


Some of Balis most underrated natural wonders are its waterfalls. The island houses some of the most beautiful falls in the world -- and with such a large selection, you can spend an entire trip exploring a handful of epic sights. Consider taking one day from the Bali waterfall route and pay a visit to secret waterfalls, some of which contain sacred, ancient water. The towering natural wonders will surely be the height of your trip and will leave an impression of a lifetime.


Bali is a beautiful island with a myriad of gorgeous sights and exhilarating experiences. There is always something new to discover, and you will find yourself frequently returning every time you miss the tropics. If you take the route less travelled, you will undoubtedly fall in love with the island over and over again.
