Garages move quickly, and even minor delays can disrupt schedules. This is why garage owners must ensure that every job receives high-quality service while consistently providing customers with clear updates. But if these tasks are handled by separate systems, it can be difficult to keep everything in sync.

A garage management platform brings these daily tasks into one single system. It allows your mechanics to check job details and plan their next steps without switching between tools. This helps reduce confusion and ensures staff are working with accurate, up-to-date information.

It also supports better use of time, as your staff can prepare proactively and avoid last-minute changes with clear job lists and part records. That’s crucial if you want to complete as many tasks as possible each day, without unnecessary delays.

Choosing garage management software that suits your garage’s workflow can help create a more organised workshop routine. With the right software, your team can focus on completing jobs with the utmost care and efficiency, rather than chasing updates.

To help you compare your options, here are 5 garage management software providers that support garages across the UK with reliable tools, allowing them to save time and effort in the workshop.

## **2. Simple GMS**

Simple GMS is a cloud-based garage management tool designed to help workshops maintain steady workflows. It brings jobs, customer records, vehicle details, and invoicing into a single browser-based system that your team can access on multiple devices.

The system includes live data services that show accurate vehicle information, MOT history and address lookups. It can also send automated messages to customers for reminders and invoices.

Simple GMS supports online bookings through your website and lets your staff update job status in real time. With all of these tools combined, this platform streamlines daily work in garages and keeps mechanics focused on providing quality service.

## **3. Motasoft**

Motasoft offers its Virtual Garage Manager (VGM) software to workshops across the UK. The system is built to help garages manage bookings, job sheets, stock and customer details in one central place, making it easier to organise day-to-day work.

The platform shows your workshop diary online and links with parts suppliers and accounting tools to reduce admin burden. It also includes features such as mechanic tracking and vehicle history logs, helping your team stay informed as jobs progress.

With over 20 years of experience, Motasoft reports that its VGM has been used by thousands of garage professionals in the UK and has processed millions of bookings.

## **4. MOT Manager**

MOT Manager is another cloud-based workshop system that has supported hundreds of UK garages since 2016\. It provides a digital diary where bookings and vehicle details can be managed without relying on paper or manual notes, making it easier for teams to track daily work.

The platform sends automatic reminders for MOT and service appointments and allows customers to book online. These bookings link directly to your workshop’s schedule and provide accurate information to anyone looking to book.

It also supports vehicle data lookups, multi-user access, estimates and invoicing, making the experience smoother for both your team and the customer. These features help garages stay organised, maintain clear communication, and keep admin manageable.

## **5. Garage Data Systems**

Garage Data Systems (GDS) offers garage management software that makes life easier for your team. The system supports a variety of daily operations, including customer records, vehicle details and job tracking. It integrates with popular accounting tools, such as Xero, Sage and QuickBooks, to keep your business data secure and accurate.

GDS also includes modules for checking sheets on mobile devices and postcode lookup. Workshops can use these tools to organise appointments and store accurate records without moving between separate applications.

The system has been in use for many years across hundreds of UK workshops, offering ongoing support during transition.
