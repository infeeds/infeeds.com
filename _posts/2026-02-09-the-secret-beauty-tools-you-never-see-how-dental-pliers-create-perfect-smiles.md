## **Types of Dental Pliers Used in Cosmetic Work**

Different beauty-focused dental procedures call for specific types of pliers. Some of the most commonly used include:

1. **Weingart pliers** – ideal for gripping and positioning orthodontic wires  
2. **Utility pliers** – used for bending and shaping fine materials  
3. **Contour pliers** – help refine crowns, veneers and aesthetic restorations  
4. **Band-removing pliers** – help ensure orthodontic adjustments are carried out safely and comfortably. 

Each type supports accuracy, comfort and refined cosmetic results.
