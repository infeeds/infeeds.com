For this Tuning Chords Used are :

G . Bm . C . D . Em

OR

For Tuning : Standard [e  . b  . G  . D  . A  . E ]

For this Tuning Chords Used are :

F# (Instead of G) . A#min (Instead of Bm) . B (Instead of C) . C (Instead of D) . D#min (Instead of Em)
