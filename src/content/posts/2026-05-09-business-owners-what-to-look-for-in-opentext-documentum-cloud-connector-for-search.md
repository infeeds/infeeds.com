## **Security Considerations When Integrating Opentext Documentum with Cloud Search**

Security should never be an afterthought when integrating a cloud connector for enterprise search. Assess the encryption standards used both in transit and at rest to ensure your data is protected from unauthorized access.

Also, scrutinize the connector's compliance with global data protection regulations such as GDPR, HIPAA, or others relevant to your industry. Compliance is not just a legal requirement but also a crucial element in maintaining customer trust. In addition, the connector should support secure access controls, allowing administrators to define robust user permissions and maintain detailed audit logs.

Finally, be proactive in implementing a strong incident response plan should any breaches or data leaks occur. This includes understanding the service level agreements (SLAs) related to security incidents and how the cloud connector provider responds to such events.
