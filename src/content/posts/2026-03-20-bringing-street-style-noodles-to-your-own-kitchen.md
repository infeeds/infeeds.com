## **Cook on High Heat for That Street Touch**

Everything shifts when you manage the temperature right. High heat from street cooks adds a hint of smoke, sharp scent too. Begin with butter in a broad pan, watch it pool and shimmer. Onions join next, then chillies tag along. Movement keeps them safe; never let them blacken at the edges.

Add some capsicum and sauté briefly. Water goes in next, followed by maggi. With the lid off, stir as it cooks. Each twist of the spoon spreads spice evenly. Heat turned high locks in bite and taste.
